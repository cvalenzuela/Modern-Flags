Flag/flaɡ/-n: a piece of cloth or similar material, typically oblong or square, attachable by one edge to a pole or rope and used as the symbol or emblem of a country.

-or-

Flag patterns are generated by:

  • Vertical Position of Flag: HDI for 2015
  • Size of Flag: Country Population for 2015
  • Amount of Stripes: Gini Coeficient for 2015
  • Colors: CO2 per capita Emmisions for 2011
